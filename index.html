8 skill areas

Logical, critical, verbal, numerical, abstract reasoning and more — grouped by skill.

Full Practice Test

A timed mock test in six short parts with a break in the middle, drawn fresh from a large question bank.

💡

Every answer explained

Miss one and a short “Why” shows the reasoning or the working — a wrong answer becomes a chance to learn.

🎮

Three ways to practice

Untimed Practice with instant feedback, a gentle Timed mode, or the full timed test.

🌿

Calm by design

No red flashes, no countdown panic. Practice has no clock at all. Timed gives about 40 seconds a question and checks in every five — look at what you missed, then keep going or stop. And a hard question can be set aside; it slides back in later, so being stuck never means being stuck.

🔖

Never lose your place

Close the tab mid-round and come back — the round is still there, at the question you left. The Full Practice Test picks up too. Practice mode has no clock, so a break costs nothing.

🎲

Fresh every time

Questions and answer choices are shuffled each run, so kids solve rather than memorize.

🖨️

Printable workbook

The whole question set as a PDF with a full answer key and a worked explanation for every question. It's for grown-ups — because it holds every answer, it stays behind your PIN and out of child mode.

🏡

Screen time, your call

Optional parent controls: a profile for each child, a daily limit from 15 minutes to 2 hours behind a PIN only you know, and a plain list of recent rounds so you can see what they actually practised.
